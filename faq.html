Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the National Kidney Foundation, our programs, and donation methods.

The National Kidney Foundation is a non-profit organization operating in Yemen to provide critical care for kidney patients, including dialysis sessions, health awareness, and support for affected families. We have been working for over 20 years across 12 medical centers throughout the country.

You can also donate via Al-Qutaybi Bank: YER: 499692652

Please send the transfer receipt via WhatsApp for follow-up.

100% of your donations go directly to patient care. Uses include:

  • Providing essential medications and medical supplies
  • Operating dialysis centers
  • Transportation support for patients
  • Awareness and early detection programs

We offer four core programs:

  • Dialysis Support: Providing equipment, supplies, and medications
  • Screening & Awareness: Regular checkups and health education
  • Family Support: Financial and social assistance for families
  • Patient Assistance: Transportation and basic medication support
