Who We Are

National Kidney Foundation

The National Kidney Foundation is a non-profit organization operating in Yemen, aiming to provide support and care for kidney patients, and to raise community awareness about kidney diseases and prevention methods.

We work through a team of volunteers and health specialists, and we strive to expand our services to reach as many patients as possible across various governorates of Yemen.

What We Believe

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To provide support and care for kidney patients in Yemen, promote health awareness, and support affected families.

Vision

A Yemeni community aware of kidney health, where every patient receives the care and support they deserve.

Values

Humanity, transparency, solidarity, and commitment. These principles guide every action we take.

What We Do

Our Core Services

We focus on three key areas to make a tangible impact in patients' lives:

  • 1
    Dialysis Support

    Providing necessary support for operating dialysis centers and securing supplies.

  • 2
    Awareness & Early Detection

    Educational programs and regular checkups for early detection of kidney diseases.

  • 3
    Family Support

    Providing material and moral support to patients' families to alleviate burdens.

Be Part of Our Mission

Whether through donating, volunteering, or spreading awareness, you can make a difference in the lives of kidney patients and their families.
